Our freight exchange solution for a higher turnover and less empty runs

TimoCom connects road hauliers, freight forwarders and companies from manufacturing and trade. There is something in for anyone: Up to 750,000 freight and vehicle offers are offered in the freight exchange by more than 127,000 users from across Europe (status quo: 12/2017) – tendency is increasing.

Your advantages:

  Helps to avoid expensive empty runs
  Up to 750,000 international vehicle and freight offers daily
  More than 127,000 users from all across Europe
  More than 516 million tons of freight per annum
  Special offer for Courier / Express / Package service
  The highest possible level of security for national and international transport thanks to TimoCom security network
  Reasonable monthly flat-rate access to our transport platform
  Find freight and offer vehicle space wherever you are via the transport barometer app
  Your data are "Hosted in Germany“

Your additional top bonus:

As a customer with the "Starter package" you can place your bids free of charge using the tendering feature on the TimoCom transport platform.

With the TimoCom freight and vehicle exchange you access for free:

  • The pan-European business directory of the transport and logistics business

TimoCom's flexibility to react is as individual as companies themselves. We offer tailor-made solutions for our transport platform, which perfectly match your specific requirements. Benefit from the individual support, TimoCom applications in 24 European languages and much more:

Closed User Group (CUG)

Companies and associations can create a Closed User Group in the freight and vehicle exchange. It is like a freight exchange within the freight exchange and at the same time acts as a lucrative business network.

Group members place their freight and vehicle offers there for the CUG members exclusively to see, before the offers are made visible in the transport exchange. This way CUG members have an information and time advantage in comparison with other companies and can find suitable offers more quickly.

What is a Closed User Group?

TC Connect – the connection to the transport platform

With the API interface TC Connect, companies can establish a connection between their own scheduling software and TimoCom's transport platform. Vehicle and freight offers will automatically be transferred from the company's internal freight forwarding software to TimoCom's freight and vehicle exchange. That saves times and money.
